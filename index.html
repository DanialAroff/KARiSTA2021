What is KARiSTA?

KARiSTA is held to witness the spirit of sportsmanship as well as the unity of friendship among UiTM staff from all branches. The Staff Sports Carnival (KARiSTA) is back for its edition of 2021 from February 4 to 10, 2021. The host for this year is UiTM Terengganu Branch. A total of 20 sport events will be contested this year such as football, netball, tenpin bowling, golf, volleball, badminton, darts, chess, and E-Sports. Any recent news can be found here.

Objectives

  • To build rapport among campus staffs.
  • To encourage campus residents to involve in sport activities.

  • To search for staffs that will represent UiTM in Kejohanan Sukan Staf Badan Berkanun (SUKANUN) 2021.
